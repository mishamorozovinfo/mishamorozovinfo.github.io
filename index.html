Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between therapy and social work services?

Therapy focuses on mental health, emotional wellbeing, and personal growth using NLP techniques. Social work services help with practical life matters: navigating government systems, benefit applications, legal documentation, case management, and advocacy with authorities.

Can you help with legal matters or replace a lawyer?

I provide case management and administrative support, including documentation preparation and coordination with lawyers. However, I am not a lawyer and cannot provide legal advice. For legal matters, I work alongside your legal representation to ensure comprehensive support.

Do you work with Danish authorities and government agencies?

Yes. As a licensed Danish social worker, I regularly coordinate with communes, job centers, housing services, family services, and other government agencies on behalf of my clients. I can attend meetings, prepare documentation, and advocate for your needs.

Can you help with immigration and residency matters?

Yes. I provide support with residency applications, family reunification cases, integration programs, and navigating immigration services. My multilingual skills (Ukrainian, Russian, Danish, English) are particularly helpful for recent immigrants.

How do the case management packages work?

Case management packages provide ongoing support for complex situations. This includes regular meetings, documentation preparation, coordination with authorities, advocacy, and long-term planning. The hours can be used flexibly based on your case needs.

What languages do you speak for legal/administrative support?

I provide services in Danish, Ukrainian, Russian, and in some cases English. This is especially important when dealing with authorities, as I can help translate and ensure you fully understand your rights and obligations.

Can I book both therapy and social work services?

Absolutely! Many clients benefit from combined support - addressing both mental health needs and practical life challenges. We can create an integrated plan that supports your wellbeing holistically.
